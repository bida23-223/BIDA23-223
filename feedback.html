Frequently Asked Questions

What is WarnerBros.com?
WarnerBros.com is your one-stop site for everything Warner Bros. - movies, television, home entertainment, games and more!
How do I get tickets to attend a live taping of a show?
If you are interested in attending a live taping of your favorite show, your best bet would be to visit www.tvtickets.com where you will find all the information you need.
Can I attend a movie premiere?
Movie premieres are generally through special invitation only.
How do I sign up for exclusive content from WB Games?
Join WBPlay to gain access to bonus content, participate in the WB Games community, unlock great interactive content and receive WB Games news.
How do I get a job with Warner Bros.?
For a list of available opportunities at our company visit Warner Bros. Careers.
I have a produced film, television or script idea. How do I submit it to Warner Bros.?
Warner Bros. Entertainment’s policy, as established by its Legal Department, prohibits considering any film, television or other story material unless it is submitted by a licensed literary agent who is signatory to the Writers Guild Of America. If you are interested in obtaining a list of accredited agents, call The Writers Guild at 323-951-4000 or email them through their website at www.wga.org.
Who do I contact for press releases and media inquiries?
For press and media inquiries, visit our Press Contacts page.
Who do I contact for clips & stills licensing inquiries?
Please review our Clip and Still Licensing page and contact our clip and still department at clipandstilldept@warnerbros.com.
Who do I contact for product licensing inquiries?
Please contact our consumer products licensing department at wbcpinquiries@warnerbros.com.
I have read through all the FAQs and I still have a question. What do I do?
If you still have a question after reading through our FAQs, please consider filling in our customer support form above.
